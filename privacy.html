MainStreet Clips Support Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

MainStreet Clips is designed to work with photos and videos you choose for your own marketing projects.

Data We Access

The app requests access to selected photos or videos only when you import media. If you choose to capture new footage, the app requests camera access and may request microphone access for recorded video audio. Imported project data, captured media, and draft exports are stored locally on the device. The app requests permission to save exports only when you choose to save a finished video to Photos.

AI Features

Initial text suggestions are generated locally in the app. This build does not send your media or business copy to a third-party AI service. If future versions add third-party AI services, the app will explain what is sent and ask before sending business content or media.

Tracking

The app does not track users across apps or websites.

Purchases

Subscriptions are not enabled in this build. If paid features are added, purchases and restore flows will use Apple's StoreKit system.

Contact

For support, visit the support page or email FreeRangeLabs@drumnbus.com.
